We’re proud to produce London Tech Week in partnership with Informa Tech and London and Partners, co-hosting the UK’s premier tech event every June.

Showcasing how tech is transforming both business and society, London Tech Week drives conversations around digital transformation, diversity, and innovation, providing the tech ecosystem a platform to come together with decision makers, influencers and leaders to drive positive change.

Fuelled by the connections and ideas forged at London Tech Week, our partnership fosters diversity-driven and sector-specific communities from every corner of the tech ecosystem, all year round.

Anchor Events

ClimateTech: Uniting tech leaders to answer the climatetech challenge.

Elevating Founders: Powering the next generation of purpose-driven tech talent.

EQL:HER: Addressing the underrepresentation of women in technology to secure an inclusive future for all.

Founders of the Future: Programmes, events and tools for future founders on their journeys to become tech entrepreneurs.

Future EdTech: Connecting the brightest educators, investors and influencers to bring about transformational change within education.

Future of Work Summit: Delivering key insights and debates on the transformative impact of technology on the changing nature of work.

Global Leaders Innovation Summit: Inspiring the most innovative thinkers through connections, using technology to build a just, equitable and resilient world.

HealthTech: Accelerating technology and innovation in healthcare.

Inspiring Fifty: Led by the team at EQL:HER, InspiringFifty is a global initiative on a mission to increase diversity in tech, with an awards ceremony celebrating tech’s top women leaders taking place every June.
