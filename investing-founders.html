Investing in Founders

Investing in our founders is ultimately an investment in our future. That’s why we’ve created an integrated network of businesses our founders can access at every stage of their journeys. By providing exceptional founders with capital, support, and access, they’ll be able to build and scale faster.

Backing The Future

We’re committed to building a support network for the next generation of maverick entrepreneurs, and our friends at firstminute capital supercharge our founders’ journeys from day one.

The home of magnetic ambition, firstminute capital is a $315m AUM venture fund investing in seed stage companies. But the fund is just the start – we’re a collective of ambitious, inspiring entrepreneurs who have joined forces to turn their visions into reality. We welcome our mavericks with open arms, providing them transformational support to build a better future.

Powering Founders to Go Further, Faster

Sometimes a brilliant idea needs a little extra rocket fuel to get it off the ground. Our incubator and accelerator programme, Founders Factory, provides just that, opening doors to a world of possibilities for ambitious founders.

Founders Factory is a global network of partners guiding, fostering and investing in entrepreneurs. Their diverse team of functional experts deliver bespoke functional support to both startups and scaleups. Plus, the programme grants its startups unparalleled access to the world’s most influential businesses and their support on everything from distribution to data, relationships, credibility and capital.

The Founders Factory network is made up of 100+ corporates, investors and founders spanning a range of stages, sectors and geographies. All of our startups enjoy ongoing access to our platform of support, including peer to peer networks and events.
