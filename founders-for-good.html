Founders For Good

We’re on a mission to power positive societal impact through the entrepreneurial innovation we enable and we pride ourselves on helping founders make the world a better place. From not-for-profit events and incubator programmes to helpful research and philanthropic advisory, we prioritise giving back and help our founders do the same.

Championing Entrepreneurship in the UK and Beyond

We want to support the next generation of entrepreneurs and our partnership with The Centre for Entrepreneurs allows us to do just that. From its comprehensive directory of accelerators and incubators, to its zero-equity leadership programme for refugee founders, CFE’s work makes entrepreneurship more accessible to more communities and more diversity means a stronger startup ecosystem and a stronger economy overall.

We’re proud to partner with CFE when it comes to researching the state of entrepreneurship in the UK and beyond, empowering founders from all backgrounds and engaging and informing policymakers and the public on how we can champion entrepreneurship for the greater good.

Paying if Forward

Born out of Founders Forum, Founders Pledge, is a community of founders, investors and VCs committed to doing good. On joining, every member makes a legally binding pledge to commit a chosen portion of their personal wealth to charity. Founders Pledge then helps them understand and elevate their social impact, offering bespoke philanthropic strategies based on the latest evidence from the sector.
Founders Pledge offers a streamlined path to impact for successful entrepreneurs, providing end-to-end giving infrastructure, charity research, and a worldwide network of experts.
